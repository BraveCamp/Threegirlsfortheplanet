Three Girls For The Planet

Changing Your Life In Simple Ways About Us

Three Girls For the Planet is on a mission to sread the word and inform you about the renewable energy sources and current environmental situations that need to change. We will do this by stating clear goals, credible information, and offering solutions/ways to change individually and as a whole, the environmental state we are in right now.

Three Girls For the Planet is composed of three girls (Madison, Lila, and Alegra) and we made this website/app as apart of Brave Camp, a coding camp for girls. We wanted to do our website on these specific issues and we hope you take away something valuable from our webite.

When we were doing research for this website we talked to Chris Harrell, who was a science teacher and now has written a few songs about science.
